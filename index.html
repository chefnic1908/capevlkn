Meet Chef Nic

From the Ocean State
to Lake Norman.

Chef Nic’s culinary style is rooted in the places and traditions that shaped her.

Raised with the vibrant flavors of her Cape Verdean heritage and inspired by Rhode Island’s seafood culture, she creates cuisine that is coastal, soulful and beautifully presented. Through Cape V of LKN, those influences come together with modern elegance—fresh New England seafood, Cape Verdean blends and spices, thoughtful detail and genuine hospitality.

Cape Verdean soul. New England coastal tradition. Lake Norman elegance.
