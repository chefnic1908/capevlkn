Meet Chef Nic

Rhode Island roots. Cape Verdean soul. A table full of love.

Chef Nic’s cooking is personal. Raised with the seafood traditions of the Ocean State and inspired by her Cape Verdean heritage, she creates food that feels vibrant, generous and deeply connected to place.

From beautifully plated private dinners to abundant celebrations, each experience is composed with intention—balancing coastal freshness, bold seasoning, organic ingredients and elegant presentation.
